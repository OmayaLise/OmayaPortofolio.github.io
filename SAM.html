SAM : Save the President

Your mission is to infiltrate the factory where the president is being held hostage. Lethal force is authorized but if the enemies reach the alarm the president will be executed. Avoid brightly lit spaces and hides your victims corpses to remains undetected.

Description

  • Student project realized by a group of four on Unreal Engine (4.27)
  • C++ and blueprint
  • Made for PC and PS4

Features

  • Third-person infiltration game 
  • Two levels available : tutorial and main level
  • Player goal : reach the President 
  • Game over : the player is shot or the alarm is activated
  • Player gameplay : moving, crounching, aiming shootting and hiding corpse
  • Custom light detection system
  • Basic enemy A.I made with Unreal behavior tree system
